Explore The Lakes

Experience the ultimate fishing adventure with our top-notch charter services on three breathtaking bodies of water: Squaw Creek Reservoir, Lake Whitney, and Lake Granbury. Discover the unique specialties and thriving fish species that each lake offers, guaranteeing an exciting and memorable fishing experience. Dive into detailed information about their layout, statistics, depth, water type, and more. Get ready to reel in unforgettable moments of joy and excitement on the water.

Squaw Creek Reservoir

This 3,275-acre impoundment, situated between Glen Rose and Granbury, is teeming with catfish and a variety of other fish species. Meet me at Squaw Creek Park at 2300 Coates Road, Granbury, Texas 76048,
conveniently located off State Highway 144 and just 45 miles southwest of Fort Worth, this fishing paradise is the perfect spot for anglers looking to hook catfish. Mark your calendars as the park is open exclusively from October to March.

Lake Granbury

Experience the thrill of fishing for striper bass on Lake Granbury! With its picturesque location on the Brazos River, just an hour southwest of Dallas/Fort Worth, Lake Granbury offers the perfect setting for a memorable fishing adventure. Spanning 8,310 acres and boasting 103 miles of captivating shoreline, this narrow lake features a maximum depth of 75 feet. Come fish with us on Lake Granbury and reel in some impressive catches.
